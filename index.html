Welcometo CK's Charity Movie Fest 2021!

CK's charity Movie Fest is a yearly event in aid of no kid hungry.org.This year our event is an 80's theme showing the highest rated movies of that decade.On this website you can find all the relevant information including which movies are being shown, a gallery of each of the movies along with additional information and a place to but tickets and also donate money to our charity.


Our event is run and organised by three people, Piere Walsh who is the founder and executive chair, Michael Dunlop who is the venue owner and Lisa Healy who is the event co-ordinator. More information regarding these people can be found on the about page through the link at the top of the page. All proceeds from ticket sales and seperate donations will be going toward our choosen charity.
